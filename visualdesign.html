REBRANDING: MerGeo

MerGeo is a local company that puts on trail runs and navigation events in Washington State. I redesigned their logos to be unified across series, appeal to the aesthetics of outdoor enthusiasts, and employ a friendly, inviting style.

BRANDING

As the Marketing Manager at MerGeo, I have branded many events to improve recognition, convey our message, and excite newcomers. Below are a few examples of my work.

Winter Trail Series

In a market full of long distance trail runs, the Winter Trail Series offers refreshingly short trail runs that provide road runners a great opportunity to try out trails. With this audience in mind, I designed a friendly hare as a mascot for the series, to convey fun, excitement, and accessibility.

Tuesday Night Trail to Grill

After the winter hare for Winter Trail Series was a huge hit, I designed another mascot to represent the Tuesday Night Trail to Grill series. Similar to the Winter Trail Series, the "TNT2G" series is meant to be an extra-social series that's great for beginners. Using a cute chipmunk in the series branding communicates the fun atmosphere one can expect at these events.

Cedar Mountain Nav Race

The navigation audience differs from the trail running audience.Navigators don't expect race medals or post-race parties, but are more interested in the quality of an event's terrain, map, and reputation.

Designing for Cedar Mountain Nav Race was a balance between capturing the feeling of a more self-reliant outdoor adventure without becoming too serious for itself or becoming too intimidating for newcomers. The tree ring provides a strong image, while color choices and custom pocket knife font provide just the right amount of mild whimsy for this audience without being too cute.
