WHAT I DO

UX DESIGN

VISUAL DESIGN

ABOUT RJ

I am currently the Marketing Manager for Meridian Geographics (MerGeo), a company that hosts Northwest Trail Runs, MerGeo Nav Races, and Street Scramble events. We're a small team, which means I put my hands on every project. I write a lot of content, design a ton of artwork, and occassionaly shoot photos and teach navigation clinics.

I discovered UX design when I was working with a small website design firm to re-design MerGeo's websites and I was being an obnoxious client, the kind that tries to do the designer's job for them. Then I realized, I could make it my job!

I'm currently enrolled in BLOC's online UX Design course, and re-designing Cascade Orienteering Club's website as my capstone project. I love ux design because I love asking questions and designing with a purpose. In fact, I have so many questions and ideas, that my instructor informed me that he re-wrote parts of the curriculum to incorporate them. I expect to graduate in the summer of 2015, when I hope to join a design team, maybe yours!

When I'm not designing I love to orienteer, ride bikes, play board games, and sample local beers.
