REBECCA JENSEN

UX, Web, and Visual Designer, Illustrator

Hello! My name is Rebecca, though you can call me 'RJ.'

I live in the wonderfully green Pacific Northwest where I love to solve problems with visual answers. My process emphasizes usability, while my end product reveals my studio art foundation.

I'm available for part-time, full-time, and freelance work. Use the Contact Form to get in touch!
