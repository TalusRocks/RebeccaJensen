ABOUT RJ

I am the Marketing Manager for Meridian Geographics (MerGeo), a company that hosts Northwest Trail Runs, MerGeo Nav Races, and Street Scramble events. We're a small team, which means I put my hands on every project. I write a lot of content, design a ton of artwork, and occassionaly shoot photos and teach navigation clinics.

I discovered UX design when I was working with a small website design firm to re-design MerGeo's websites, and I realized that I wanted to do their job from a usability standpoint.

I am currently enrolled in BLOC's online UX Design course, and re-designing Cascade Orienteering Club's website as my capstone project. I expect to graduate in the summer of 2015.
